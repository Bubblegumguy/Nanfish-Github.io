Star Platnium is a close-ranged stand with abnormal strength, speed, precision, and accuracy. With its extordinay physical power and Jotaro's fighting spirit, Star Platnium is one of the strongest stands in the serires. Its also similar to the stand, "The World"

appearance

Star Platnium appearance looks more humanoid than the others, a buff, tall, similar to their user, Jotaro Kujo. Its skin has various colors throughtout the body with a hue between purple, blue, green, and a tad of gold

It has long, flowing black hair with a headband accessory with a gem in the middle. It also wears a dark purple cap on its chin, a short circual scarf, shoulder pads with a sirl design, gloves with studs on the back of the hand,

knee/elbow guards, knne length boots, and a loincloth.

Abilitys

ORA Barrage

Star Platnium does a barrage of fast punches/kicks inflicting heavy damage to the opponent.

Star Finger

Star platnium extends his middle and index finger to stab their oppenet from afar. This ability is only used when the user is immobilized

Time Stop

In his prime (Part 4) Star Platnium could stop time for 5 secounds. In part 5 he could only stop time for 0.5 - 2 secounds. At the start of part 6 he could time stop for 2 secounds but at the end it extended to 5 secounds.

Similar to The Worlds time stop, Jotaro Kujo can freely move and the objects he touches.
