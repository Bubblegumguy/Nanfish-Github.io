Magician's Red is a stand of Muhammad Avdol, its a humanoid stand with a bird as its head. It can manipulate scorching hot flames in variouse ways to defeat his eneimies

Appearance

Magician's Red is a hybrid of a human and a phoenix bird head, its upper-body is very well built, Its wrist and entire legs are wrapped with dark flames (the legs are feathered when not in flames), and has claws instead of nails. Orginally the Stand was barefooted but later wears Aabian footwear.

Abilitys

Cross Fire Hurricane

Magician's Red fires a few fireball projectiles shaped like nakhs from its mouth in a great storm. It can also spilt the ankhs to cover a wider area. Avdol was able to maneuver these projectiles for sneak attacks by manipualting individual flames

Red Bind

Magician's Red can imboilize his opponent by tying them like a rope with his flames. If his flames are close to the eneimes face, the oxygen around it will evuntally be burned, thus suffocating them.

Life Detector

Magician's Red produces a cruciform mass of flames poiting north, west, south, east, up, and down with flames at each tip. These flames can detect movement, breathing, and stan energy within a 15 meter radius. When it detectts something the flames corresponding direction will grow bigger
