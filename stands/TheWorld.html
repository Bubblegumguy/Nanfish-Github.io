The World is a close-ranged stand similar to "Star Platnium". It possess the same physical strength, speed, precision, and accuracy as Star Platnium. But with its time stopping power, it is truly a stand to be feared.

Appearance

The World is a well-built humanoid figure resembling DIO himself and his clothes.

It has a yellow slanted headwear covering its face with a heart shaped cap on its chin. Behind the stand, it has 2 diving clylinders connected to its mask, it has ridged straps over its shoulder from the front and back. The world wears fingerless gloves with beads on each finger except for the thumb. behind the hand is a clock face symbolizing its power to stop time.

Abilitys

Stop Time

This may be only one ability but it is also very powerful.

The World is cable of stopping time for only 5 secounds due to his weakened state but after sucking Joestar blood his Time Stop increases to 9 secounds

During stop time, DIO is able to move freely including the object he touches and throws. Another feature of time stop is being able to levitate. Since they're is not gravity forces pulling him down, DIO can freely move anywhere as he wishes.

With this powerful and unstoppable power DIO can strike defenseless enemies, easily doge attacks, and have an escape plan whenever he needs to.

MUDA Barrage

Similar to Star Platnium, The World unleashes a fast barrage of devastating punches/kicks that can severely injury their opponent. The Worlds physical strength and speed is quite similar, in fact even slighty better, though it is unconfirmed.
