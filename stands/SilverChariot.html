Silver Chariot is a Stand of Jean Pierre Polnareff, equipped with armor and weilds a rapier as its weapon. A deadly foe to battle in close range due to its speed and precision even being able to slice a bullet.

Appearance

Silver Chariot takes a thin robotic humanoid form in steel medieval armour, equipped with a cup-hilted rapier. It has thick, spikey shoulder pads and a helmet with a jewel embedded in the forehead. After the fight with Diavolo, Silver Chariot loses it legs and right forearm, but are replaced with metal spike-like appendages.

Abilitys

Hora Rush

With incredible speed and precision, Silver Chariot can incapacitate his opponents or slice objects with ease

Armor Removal

Silver Chariot simply sheds off its armor for increased speed, on the downside it is more vulnerable to attacks that can cause fatal damage.

Sword Launch

This ability launches his sword with great force and precision enough to pierce a human body. It can ricochet off walls or object as well. After launch Silver Chariot would have to retrieve the weapon before using it again.
