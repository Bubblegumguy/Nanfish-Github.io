Hermit Purple is the stand of Joseph Joestar. A vine-like stand summoned from their hands, mainly used to divine information and messages from other locations

appearance

Hermit purple is a thorn-covered vine in the color of purple. Multiple vines come out when spawned from the hand that covers Josephs hand. Its strong enough to support his own weight and bind someone as well.

Abilitys

Divination

Hermit Purple can aquire information by using objects or material to gain info. Joseph smashed a camera to show a photograph of a person or even a place. He then used it on a tv being able to broadcast a persons thought or film a place.

Eletronic Manipulation

Aside from gathering information, Hermit Purple can also tamper with eletronics. Using he's ability, he was able to take control of a Cessna's system, forcing it to stabilize to prevent crashing.
